Bart Bouwens

About Me

Hi there, my name is Bart Bouwens. I am 22 years old and I study Multimedia- & Communication Technology-Virtual Production at Karel De Grote University College in Hoboken. I am someone who likes to entertain people by making them laugh, telling them stories, ect... . During my studies I have found animation, both 2D and 3D, very useful to deliver my, sometimes absurd, sense of humour. I believe that technology is the key, or at least one of the factors, to a better and brighter future for our society.

What I Do
Unity Developer

I have made several small projects in Unity, mostly 2D games. But there are so many more possibilities within the unity engine. I am currently researsching Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality for these two technologies are already developing at a hi-speed pace and I have no doubt that they will be of great help in our future.

3D Artist

3D modeling is one of my favourite things to do, I my spare time I tend to learn how to model better. I am currently learning how to rig and animate a character in cinema4D. I am also learning to use Maya

Voice Acting

Although I have not done this often yet, I really do enjoy voice acting roles. I find it relaxing. I guess you could say that I like the sound of my own voice.

UI/UX Design

While I do mainly focus on 3D and virtual technology, I also have some knowledge in UI/UX Design. I use this mostly for creating icons for my games and apps. For this I use Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.

Animals

And now for something completely different, aside from technology my greatest passion is animals. I do not work with them but i do know a lot about them, well some of them at least. Since they are my main source of inspiration for creating 3D content I do learn about them in my free time, when I am not wasting my time with gaming at least.

Movies

Something that I really like to do is telling stories. Currently I have only made several short videos. But I have plans to make something bigger.
